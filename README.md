# Currency Exchange Locator

A simple project that fulfilled the requirements of our Data Structure and Algorithm course. This utilizes java and java swing.

---

## Features

* **Interactive Map Interface**: Click anywhere on the city map to set your location. The app instantly recalculates relative distances to nearby money changers.
* **Multi-Currency Support**: Exchange multiple currencies at once (USD, EUR, JPY, GBP, HKD, SGD, SAR, AED, KRW, TWD). The app calculates the total PHP value based on live (simulated) rates.
* **Smart Sorting & Filtering**: Sort results by Closest, Best Value, or Highest Rated. Filter to only show shops that are Open Now.
* **Dark/Light Mode**: A fully responsive modern UI that toggles between a clean Light theme and a sleek Dark theme.
* **Digital Receipt Generation**: Generates a clean, HTML-based transaction summary/invoice detailing your selected provider, exchange rates, and total value.
* **Broad City Coverage**: Includes localized data for Valenzuela City, Malabon City, Caloocan City, Quezon City, Manila City, and Bulacan Province.

---

## Application Flow

1. **Input Page**: Enter your name, contact, city, and the currencies you wish to exchange.
2. **Map & Results Page**: View your results on a map. Click the map to drop your location pin. The sidebar highlights the top matches based on your criteria. Hover over pins to see projected exchange values.
3. **Summary Page**: Once you find the best deal, click "View Receipt" to generate a digital invoice.
